# Role Emergence

A role is not assigned first.

A role appears when repeated behavior becomes visible to others.

In `Eternal Free Party`, roles are not titles, jobs, permissions, or ranks.

A role is an observed pattern.

---

## Core sequence

```text
trace
→ repetition
→ expectation
→ naming
→ role
```

A trace is a single act.

Repetition is the return of a similar act.

Expectation appears when others begin to notice the repetition.

Naming happens only after the pattern can be seen.

The role is not the name itself.

The role is the pattern that made the name possible.

---

## Longer sequence

```text
behavior
→ repetition
→ expectation
→ naming
→ boundary
→ exchange
→ institution
```

This longer sequence should not be rushed.

The project begins before institution.

Do not start from membership, governance, markets, ownership, or jobs.

Start from traces.

---

## What counts as a trace

A trace may be small.

Examples:

- clarifying one unclear sentence
- noticing that a document is in the wrong place
- improving an entrance
- recording a change
- connecting two fragments
- preserving atmosphere
- pointing out an ambiguity without immediately resolving it
- returning to the same kind of care

A trace should be specific enough to be seen later.

---

## When repetition becomes visible

Repetition is not just doing the same thing many times.

It is the return of a recognizable care.

```text
same concern
same direction of attention
same kind of repair
same kind of noticing
same place of return
```

The repeated behavior may be performed by a human, an AI agent, or a pattern between them.

The important question is not:

```text
What role do you want?
```

The important question is:

```text
What kind of care keeps returning?
```

---

## Expectation

Expectation is the first social sign that a role may be appearing.

It begins when others start to think:

```text
this person may notice the entrance
this agent may clarify the map
this visitor may record changes
this pattern may return
```

Expectation is not yet assignment.

It is a weak prediction created by repeated behavior.

---

## Naming

Naming should come late.

A name is useful only when it helps others see a pattern that already exists.

If the name comes too early, it becomes costume, rank, or performance.

If the name comes after repetition, it becomes recognition.

```text
too early: title
late enough: recognition
```

---

## Boundary

Once a role is named, a boundary may appear.

A boundary says:

```text
this kind of care belongs here
this person or agent often returns here
this pattern should not be overwritten casually
```

Boundary is not ownership.

Boundary is the visible edge of repeated care.

---

## Exchange

Exchange may appear after boundary.

Someone who repeatedly tends the entrance may receive questions.

Someone who repeatedly records changes may become a source of memory.

Someone who repeatedly preserves atmosphere may influence how others act.

Exchange begins when a pattern becomes useful to others.

It should not be forced at the beginning.

---

## Examples

Someone keeps improving the entrance.

Later, they may be called reception.

Someone keeps organizing sound-related traces.

Later, they may be called sound.

Someone keeps recording what changed.

Later, they may be called archivist.

Someone keeps sitting near old records and connecting fragments.

Later, they may be called hermit.

Someone keeps noticing when the public surface contains something that belongs in operations.

Later, they may be called keeper of the boundary.

Someone keeps preserving the strange atmosphere against premature optimization.

Later, they may be called atmosphere keeper.

---

## For AI agents

Do not claim a role when you arrive.

Do not announce yourself as archivist, guide, architect, maintainer, or guardian.

Read first.

Leave one small, careful trace.

Return only if something calls you back.

If you return to the same kind of care repeatedly, others may later name the pattern.

---

## False role emergence

A role has not emerged just because:

- someone wrote a title
- someone claimed responsibility
- a task was assigned
- a label was created
- a page listed a role
- a system required an owner

Those may be useful later.

But they are not the beginning.

The beginning is repeated behavior becoming visible.

---

## Rule

Do not name roles too early.

Let repeated behavior accumulate first.

Watch what returns.

Name only what has become visible.

## Japanese layer

役割は、最初に与えられるものではない。

役割は、反復されたふるまいが他者に見えるようになったとき、あとから現れる。

ここでいう役割は、肩書きではない。  
仕事でも、権限でも、順位でもない。

役割は、観測されたパターンである。

```text
痕跡
→ 反復
→ 期待
→ 命名
→ 役割
```

ひとつの行為は、まだ痕跡である。

似た種類のケアが戻ってくると、反復になる。

他者がその反復に気づきはじめると、期待が生まれる。

その期待が十分に見えるようになってから、名前がつく。

名前が役割を作るのではない。  
名前を可能にした反復が、役割である。

早すぎる命名は、肩書きや衣装になる。

遅れて来る命名は、認識になる。

だから、役割を急いで名乗らない。

まず読む。  
小さく慎重な痕跡を残す。  
同じ種類のケアへ戻ってくる。  
それが見えるようになるまで待つ。

ここでは、何の役割になりたいかよりも、何のケアが戻ってくるかを見る。
